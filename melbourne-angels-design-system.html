Design System

A comprehensive visual identity system for Melbourne Angels' 2026 website refresh. This system defines the colours, typography, components, and patterns that create a cohesive, modern, and credible digital presence — one that signals tech-literacy, quality, and community.

Tech-literate, not corporate

Feel native to the tech and startup ecosystem. Avoid legacy corporate or association aesthetics.

Confident without hype

Quality, structure, and content signal sophistication. No buzzwords or gimmicks.

Human and community-led

Balance modern design with warmth. A member-driven group, not a faceless institution.

Typography

A two-font system: Inter for headings and UI elements, DM Sans for body text and long-form content. Both are open-source Google Fonts with excellent readability and a modern, tech-native feel.

Heading Font — Inter

Inter is a variable-weight sans-serif designed for screen readability. Its geometric precision and wide weight range make it ideal for confident, modern headlines — similar to what Startmate uses, but with more warmth.

Body Font — DM Sans

DM Sans is a geometric sans-serif with slightly softer curves than Inter, giving body text a warm, approachable feel while maintaining excellent readability at smaller sizes.

Voice & Tone

How Melbourne Angels sounds. Credible and grounded, supportive but selective, collaborative and expert, clear and direct.

Do
"We back founders building scalable technology businesses, typically post-revenue with strong product-market fit."
Don't
"We're a super exciting group of passionate angel investors who love disrupting industries and changing the world!"
Do
"Our members bring decades of experience across technology, healthcare, and financial services."
Don't
"Our world-class, premier, award-winning investors are the best in the business."
Do
"Not sure if your startup is the right fit? Review our investment criteria before submitting."
Don't
"Only the most exceptional founders should apply. We have extremely high standards."

Accessibility

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance targets. Key contrast ratios and accessibility considerations for the design system.

CombinationContrast RatioWCAG AAUsage
900 on White15.4:1✓ PassPrimary body text
600 on White5.7:1✓ PassSecondary text
White on Blue 5004.8:1✓ PassPrimary buttons
White on Orange 5003.1:1⚠ Large text onlyUse 700 for small text
White on 90015.4:1✓ PassDark sections, footer
Note on Orange (#F68812)
The brand orange does not meet WCAG AA contrast ratio (4.5:1) for normal-weight text on white backgrounds. For small text, use Orange 700 (#94520B) instead. Orange 500 is safe for large text (18px+ bold or 24px+ regular), buttons with white text, and decorative/eyebrow elements.
