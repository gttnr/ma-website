For Investors

A community built to
invest better, together

Melbourne Angels is member-run. Everyone contributes — to deal sourcing, due diligence, and portfolio support. In return, you get access to opportunities and expertise you couldn't find alone.

1
Meet the community
Attend a Monthly Meeting and a Networking Lunch. Get referred by a current member — this is a membership community built on trust and shared purpose.
2
Submit your application
Share your CV and meet the Membership Team. We look for investors with experience, appetite for risk, and a genuine desire to help founders win.
3
Start evaluating deals
Three to five new companies present each month. You choose what interests you and invest on your own terms — no mandates, no minimums beyond one deal per year.
4
Help portfolio companies succeed
Contribute to boards, mentor founders, leverage your network. Your experience is as valuable as your capital to the founders we back.

For Founders

What we look for in a deal

We've backed 150+ companies and run 700+ individual investment rounds. We know what great looks like — and we invest with conviction when we see it.

Our members have deep executive and entrepreneurial experience across every sector. One of the core benefits of working with Melbourne Angels is the active coaching and network those investors provide.

Submit a Deal → Read our investment criteria
Stage
Seed and beyond. Working prototype, customer discovery done, business model clear.
Market Opportunity
Large, accessible market. Credible path to $20m+ revenue within 3–5 years.
Team
Ethical, passionate, deeply experienced. Diversity of thinking. Open to coaching.
IP & Defensibility
IP at the heart of your advantage. Sustainable differentiation for each stakeholder in your channel.
Exit Strategy
Patient 5–8 year investors seeking 10x from a strategic trade sale. You should have a view.
Financial Projections
Detailed, granular P&L, balance sheet and cash flow. Month-by-month year one. No hand-waving.
